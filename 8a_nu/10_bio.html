8a Questionnaire
What do you like the most in your climbing life?To rapell/bolt down finding and touching new terrain and the holds, myself and my friends will climb and enjoy. To start working and doing the new sequences and invite my friends.
 
What is a memorable story in your climbing life?I was coaching the 16-year-old Said Belhaj to clip higher up on a 8b FA. Dyno, dyno, dyno and he has got the unclipped carabiner under his feet. Feeling kind of stupid, I see how he bends down and lifts up the carabiner with his right foot and managed to squeeze it with his left and then he reaches for the carabiner, clipping and makes the FA! WOW!
 
Who are your climbing heroes and why?For me it is Dani Andrada for putting up so many routes and always be so nice an encouraging at the crags. Sharma for his passion to focus long times putting up and create history at the same time, without saying anything. Ondra for his tallent and also for being so brave, giving his opinions and downgrade. It would have been so much easier for him if he did not give all his comments. I do think these are three perfect role models for the climbing community.
