8a Questionnaire
What is a memorable story in your climbing life?My first day of outdoor climbing in Norway - a bikini and a pair of pink climbing shoes in a tiny city handbag. The sun was hot on the seacliff; experienced climbers stayed at home complaining about horrible friction. I smeared and edged on miniature holds. Rubber and chalk melting into the wall. Using no force at all, I danced my way up to the top, one route after the other, as if the rock was a part of me. From that day onwards, I always knew I could fly.
