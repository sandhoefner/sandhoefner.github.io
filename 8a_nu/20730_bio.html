8a Questionnaire
What do you like the most in your climbing life?The climbing world is such a unique environment. My favorite thing about it is that there is always something different to challenge you. In a lot of sports you meet the same obstacles. In climbing you can always try a different route, a different problem. It never gets old.
 
What is a memorable story in your climbing life?I went to Hueco Tanks in march 2008. I went with a friend who used to guide there and climbed back in the 90s in the Nicole/Sherman era. He is now a doctor and has 4 kids so he doesn't climb as much. We went out there and we climbed a bunch of stuff and we ended up on Babyface 7B/V7. He had tried it many times but he hadn't ever done it. On his 8th try of the day he sent. It was just cool because I got to see what climbing was all about. Pushing your own limits.
