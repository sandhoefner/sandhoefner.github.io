It goes quite well and so I decide to give it a try in the other one, Chromosome Y, and well it works out, too. After a summer with only some bouldering I am retaking some steps towards route climbing. "Le lézard communiste" (8c/8c+) at the Jansegg, near the Gastlosen in Fribourg/Switzerland is the first of them. It took me about 15 tries to send this line of outstanding rock and crazy moves on the incomparable "shield" up at 1800 meters.
