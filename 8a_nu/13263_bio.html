8a Questionnaire
What do you like the most in your climbing life?The climbing community. The ability to call up any friends and be able to go sesh in the gym or at the local crag.
 
What is a memorable story in your climbing life?Placing 5th in Difficulty and and 3rd in Speed at 2009 SCS Youth Nationals in SLC, Utah; and later getting to represent the US Youth National Team at Youth World Championships in Valence, France. And climbing at Maple Canyon with Zero Gravity.
 
Who are your climbing heroes and why?Anyone who gives back towards the climbing community.
 
Which new or unknown crag/destination could you recommend? Please give some details.Nothing really new that I have climbed at.
 
Which climbing destinations could you recommend and why?I recommend Squamish, BC for Bouldering and Big Wall/Multipitch climbing. And Maple Canyon for good, legit ropes.
 
Top-5 Climber of 2008?1. Adam Ondra - For pushing climbing to it's limits. For opening up new problems and routes giving hope to the youth climbers of the world. And for setting new standards and putting out a new realm of possibility for the future of climbing. 2. Julian Bautista - For pushing the limits of youth climbing in America. For giving back to the climbing community and showing the US/world what a real climber is all about. 3. Alex Puccio - Continuing to take the female climbing world to new levels. And also for giving back to the climbing community. 4. Anna Stohr - Along with Alex. Also for crushing the competition circuits around the world. 5. I will think of someone else.
