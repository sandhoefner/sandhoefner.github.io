It's probably about time I wrote something... Hi I'm Zoe. I have been climbing since the end of 2012. I started in England in a gym in Plymouth and got tonnes of flappers and new nothing about climbing. I then moved back home and climb at Pulse now in Newcastle. But I don't live in Newcastle. I started outdoor bouldering mid 2013. Maybe a bit later -- don't remember. I wish I liked trad or sport climbing because I think they look nice until I get attached to a rope. Truth is I love bouldering too much. I've done a few comps. And I feel like I have spent almost every weekend this year climbing boulders in Sydney that I have really got no where on.
