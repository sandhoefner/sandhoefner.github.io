8a Questionnaire
What do you like the most in your climbing life?The people. Climbing is a great vehicle for meeting new and interesting folks. All of my dearest friends I met through climbing.
 
What is a memorable story in your climbing life?I went ice climbing in Cody, WY with my dear friend Bobby Model (who was in a terrible accident in South Africa). He wanted to shoot photos and he soloed ahead of the rest of us. When I started up the climb, which was in a narrow chimney, Bobby yelled down "hey Leif, turn around and open your mouth". He then started to pee all over the climb above me from 100 feet up, it was super nasty and I couldn't go anywhere. I will miss his old sense of humor.
 
Who are your climbing heroes and why?Todd Skinner, Fred Nicole, Scott Milton. They've all taught me to believe in myself.
 
Which new or unknown crag/destination could you recommend? Please give some details.Tensleep Canyon in Wyoming is truly the dope shit, go there and discover! Despite the short season and long approach, Wall Rock in the Big Horns is a well kept secret GEM! Bring your bolt gun.
 
Which climbing destinations could you recommend and why?Baldwin Creek in Lander, if you need a reason, you don't need to go there. Sitting Bull Falls New Mexico, best little crag I've been to anywhere! Terradets/Bruixes Spain, honest to God tufas, for 35 meters.
