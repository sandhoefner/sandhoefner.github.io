868c0acd13-10-14 868c0acdInto Darkness Luskville FA868c0acd 868c0acdThe temple super project. I have been working on this line for the past 3 years. When I came back form Colorado 2 years ago I manage to figure all the moves but, I didnt have the stamina to do the full line in one go.Yesterday I found new beta that I didnt see before that made the climb a bit easier. The problem start at the back of the dihedral where you have to do a big first move to a 1 finger crack pinch. After that You have to bring your feet up and bump your left hand to a bad pinch( the Crux) Then you have do another big move with the left to a undercling and work your feet out of the dihedral. I then cam my left foot on a good edge and go full extention on a good edge with the left hand. You then finish the climb on some good and easier climbing to the left. ***868c0acd
